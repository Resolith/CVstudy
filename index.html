Attention Allocation in Hiring Decisions:
An Eye-Tracking Study of Role Influence

Informed Consent

Study Purpose: This study investigates eye movement patterns during hiring decision-making tasks. Your participation will help us understand attention patterns using eye-tracking technology.

What you will do: You will review candidate profiles and make hiring decisions in two different scenarios. Your eye movements will be tracked using your webcam while you view the materials. The study takes approximately 12-15 minutes to complete.

Voluntary Participation: Your participation is completely voluntary. You may withdraw from the study at any time without penalty. There are no known risks associated with participation in this study.

Technical Requirements:

  • A functioning webcam with clear view of your face
  • Good lighting conditions (avoid backlighting or shadows on your face)
  • Stable internet connection
  • A comfortable, stationary position where you can remain still during tasks
  • Note: The study will automatically enter fullscreen mode for optimal display and eye-tracking accuracy

Privacy and Data Protection:

  • All data collected will be anonymized and used solely for research purposes.
  • Webcam data is processed locally on your device and never stored, transmitted or recorded.
  • You can leave the study at any time by closing the browser window.

By clicking "Agree and Participate", you confirm that you:

  • Are at least 18 years old
  • Have read and understood the information above
  • Voluntarily agree to participate in this study.
  • Consent to the collection of anonymous eye-tracking and demographic data

Generate Your Anonymous Code

To help us analyze the data accurately without collecting personal information, we ask you to generate a short, anonymous code. This allows us to group your data while keeping you fully anonymous. You do not need to remember or save this code.

  1. What is the first letter of your mother's first name?
  2. What is the first letter of your father's first name?
  3. How many older brothers do you have? (If none, enter 0)
  4. How many older sisters do you have? (If none, enter 0)
  5. Does your first name begin with a letter from A–M or N–Z?
    • If A–M, enter: first
    • If N–Z, enter: second
  6. What month were you born in? (Please write the full name of the month, for example: March)
  7. What is the first letter of your middle name? If you do not have a middle name, enter N.

Now combine your answers into one line, in this order:
mother's initial, father's initial, number of older brothers, number of older sisters, name half (first or second), birth month, middle name initial.

Example: M, J, 1, 2, first, March, A → becomes: MJ12firstMarchA
